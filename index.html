Workflow

Built around how you actually post.

You still edit inside TikTok. This just handles the boring reposting layer after TikTok saves the video to your phone.

01

Post on TikTok

Edit, caption and publish exactly as normal. Let TikTok save the final MP4 to your camera roll.

02

Upload saved video

Drop the saved MP4 into Repurpose from your phone or desktop. The layout works cleanly on both.

03

Paste share link

Paste the TikTok link. The backend will detect the caption, then you can edit it before posting.

04

Queue everywhere

Send the uploaded MP4 and caption to connected accounts like YouTube Shorts and Instagram Reels.
